Honey Mustard Crispy Chicken Wrap

Description

Indulge in the delightful combination of flavors and textures with our Honey Mustard Crispy Chicken Wrap. This recipe features succulent chicken tenders, coated in a crispy golden crust, paired with a vibrant mix of fresh greens, ripe tomatoes, and crunchy cucumbers. Each wrap is generously drizzled with a tangy honey mustard sauce, balancing sweetness with a hint of sharpness. All these ingredients are snugly enveloped in a warm, soft tortilla, creating a satisfying and portable meal perfect for lunch or a light dinner. Easy to prepare and irresistibly delicious, this wrap is sure to become a favorite in your household.

Ingedients

  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup panko crumbs
  • 0.5 teaspoons salt
  • 0.25 teaspoons pepper
  • 0.5 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 0.5 teaspoons onion powder
  • 8 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 2 cups chredded lettuce
  • 1 cup red grapes, halved
  • 0.5 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 0.3 cup sliced almonds
  • 6 sun-dried tomato tortillas

Steps

  1. Pound chicken breast until about 0.5 inches thick
  2. Lightly beat egg in a shallow bowl
  3. Combine panko crumbs, salt, pepper, garlic powder and onion powder in a separate shallow bowl
  4. Dip chicken breasts in egg until coated, drip off excess, and dip in panko mixture
  5. Press chicken into crumbs until fully coated
  6. Heat oil in a large skillet on medium high heat. Transfer chicken to pan and cook about 2 to 3 minutes per side or until chicken is brown, crispy and fully cooked
  7. Transfer chicken to a plate and cut into stripes
  8. Combine desired amount of chicken, honey mustard, bacon, lettuce, grapes, shredded cheddar cheese, green onions and sliced almonds in tortillas
