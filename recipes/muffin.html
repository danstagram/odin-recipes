Banana Oat Muffin

Description

Start your day with a wholesome treat by baking a batch of Banana Oat Muffins. These muffins are a perfect blend of ripe bananas and hearty oats, creating a moist and tender crumb with a delightful nutty flavor. Infused with a hint of cinnamon and sweetened naturally with honey, these muffins are both nutritious and delicious. Ideal for a quick breakfast on-the-go or a satisfying snack, each bite offers a burst of banana goodness complemented by the comforting texture of oats. Easy to make and even easier to enjoy, Banana Oat Muffins are sure to become a favorite in your home.

Ingedients

  • 1.5 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 0.5 cups white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 egg
  • 0.75 cup milk
  • 0.3 cup vegetable oil
  • 0.5 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup mashed bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (205 degrees C). Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. Combine flour, oats, sugar, baking powder, soda, and salt in a medium bowl; set aside.
  3. Beat egg lightly in a large bowl. Whisk in milk, oil, and vanilla. Stir in mashed bananas. Add the flour mixture and stir until just combined. Spoon batter into the prepared muffin cups, filling each 3/4 full.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until tops spring back when lightly pressed, 18 to 20 minutes.
