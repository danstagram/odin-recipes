Pancake

Description

This is a bulk whole wheat pancake mix recipe that my family loves, and it is very easy to make! It also makes good waffles.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups whole wheat flour
  • 3 cups unbleached all-purpose flour
  • 0.5 cup white sugar
  • 2.5 tablespoons baking powder
  • 4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 4 tablespoons salt

Steps

  1. Whisk whole what flour, all-purpose flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt together in a bowl
  2. Mix 1 cup of pancake mix, 1 cup of milk, 1 egg, and 2 tablespoons of oil together in a large bowl
  3. Cook pancake on a hot griddle
  4. Add toppings of your choice
